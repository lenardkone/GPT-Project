This is an Introductory Web Experience exploring Digital Meditation Practices by utilising GPT-3 & Neural Speech Synthesis.

Once you enter you will be prompted with a text box in which you can describe your current state of mind, an area in your life that demands attention, a specific situation or topic you would like to meditate about.

After a short wait you will receive a guided meditation back aimed at beginners based on your desired request.
You can further personalise the experience with our built-in theme selector.
